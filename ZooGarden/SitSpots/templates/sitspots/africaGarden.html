Anna's Hummingbird

Male//photo by Kyle Blaney

female//photo by Ryan Winkleman

Description : Tiny green and gray bird, with a straght short bill and a fairly broad tail. Which extend longer than the wingtips.

Diet: Nectar from th flowering plants including current and gooseberry nectar are the main diet. They also can be found to eat a variety of insects, the smaller ones ones that can be found on a web or flower.

Habitat: Common in urban and suburban settings. Other habitas they can be found in are chaparral,costal srubs and open woodlands.
